MEMORIES

My project,"Memories", is a cinematic video, with several still shots, that would give off a very cinematic ambiance. The video tell the story of places experienced by "certain people" or minds, and show the mystery of forgotten memories. That's why my project is called "Memories", as it allows me to have a vast idea of shots and composition.
A large proportion of the shots is completely natural and already composed, but there i also be shots composed by myself or I have my own scenario, like a scene from a film.
