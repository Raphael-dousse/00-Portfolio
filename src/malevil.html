MALEVIL

The "Malevil" project is a rap video with music by French artist Nekfeu.
The song is about the city's viciousness between people and our everyday lives. For this project, the main intention is to show the strength of solitude and the principles of a rap clip with rhythms, but with a story in the background. The strength of this project lies in the whole atmosphere that this protagonist shows us throughout the clip.


(Eikon) School project in collaboration with a classmate - October 2022
