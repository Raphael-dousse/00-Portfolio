MALEVIL

"Malevil" is music video project realized in the framework of my schoolwork. It is based on the song by French rap artist Nekfeu. Parting from the lyrics of the song, the objective was to paint the atmosphere of the song and to imagine different scenographic settings for the video clip.
The strength of this project lies in the general atmosphere that the protagonists release throughout the clip.


(Eikon) School project in collaboration with a classmate - October 2022
