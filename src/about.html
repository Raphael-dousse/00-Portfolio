WHAT ABOUT ME ?

My name is Raphaël Dousse, I am an 18-year old interactive media designer student at Eikon, a renowned applied arts school in Fribourg, Switzerland.

I'm passionate about cinema, video and photography production, but I also have experiences in web design and graphic design.

I do a lot of post-production and I work on personal projects such as music videos, cinematic videos, urban photography and many other projects. What I really like about this job is shooting, because there's nothing more satisfying than freezing a picture in time.
