Moutains is a personal photography project I realized in Bellegarde (German: Jaun), a Swiss town and commune in the canton of Fribourg, located in the Gruyère district.
The mountains in the images are the Gastlosen, a 15-kilometre-long limestone chain in the Fribourg Pre-Alps.
