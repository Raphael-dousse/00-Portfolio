GLITCH

"Glitch" is a collaborative short motion project run by students at EIKON. Each student had to realize two short Motion 3D sequences, which were put together in the final video project accompanied with music.
The latter was edited in After Effect, and includes split screens, transitions and various AE effects that can enhance the efficiency and impact of raw footages.


(Eikon) School project with students - October 2023
