GLITCH

Glitch is a collaborative project with students from the school. Each student had to make two short Motion 3D sequences, so as to be able to create a single video with all the students' sequences. The video, edited in After Effect, was to include split screens, transitions and various AE effects that could increase the impact of the raw footage tenfold.
Various glitch effects are used on several shots, or even on the whole sequence. Because is the subject of the project.
