MANTECA

"Manteca" is music video project Produced for my end of the year project in the Interactive Media Designer (eikon) course. It is based on the song Manteca by Dizzy Gillespie. Performed by the Big Band of the Fribourg Conservatory and based on a dynamic style that accompanies the music, the video takes a step back into the world of music with a classic band rehearsal.
The project was inspired by the film called "Whiplash" by Damien Chazelle.
