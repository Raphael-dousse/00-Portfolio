BIGFOOT ?

“Bigfoot” is a personal self-written project situated between the fictional documentary and the mockumentary.
It humorously engages with the pseudoscience of cryptozoology and a creature called Bigfoot. It is an imagined tale of the legends around Bigfoot in the Grandfey region in Switzerland. The main character of the film, Nicolas Müller, is a cryptozoologist who passionately researches about Bigfoot.
