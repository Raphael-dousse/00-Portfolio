BIGFOOT ?

This documentary is about cryptozoology and a creature called Bigfoot. These legends are lies, but some truth must be told, as cryptozoology is a profession. That's why my project is scripted, but reveal some real facts about the world of cryptozoology.
Nicolas Müller is a created character who works in cryptozoology and is passionate about bigfoot. He is sent to Fribourg to investigate a sighting of the creature in the Grandfey forest.
