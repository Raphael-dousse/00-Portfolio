MOUNTAINS

“Mountains” is a personal photography project, realized in the mountain chain of the “Gastlosen” at Bellegarde, a Swiss town and commune in the canton of Fribourg.
The mountains are a 15-kilometre-long limestone chain in the pre-Alps culminating at 2252 meters.
