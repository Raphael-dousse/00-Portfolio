27 SWISS ALPS

"27 Swiss Alps" is a personal video project, from the airplane rushes of MEMORIES. I simply had fun and made this video to create a cinematic atmosphere with its fantastic shots over the Swiss Alps. Color grading can make shots look amazing ! (Thanks DaVinci)
Radiohead's “Everything in Its Right Place” is a perfect fit, sounding great in the background, and I was a little inspired by Gareth Edwards' film “The Creator”.
